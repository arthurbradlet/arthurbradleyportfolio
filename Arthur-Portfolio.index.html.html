About Me

A couple of years ago you would've seen me playing full time football at a professional football club. But none the less Web Development had always interested me. I thought to myself how do they do it? A few years went by after stopping football completely I honestly wondered what I would do with my life.

After roles within Retail Management, I found myself still not knowing what I wanted to build a career in. After speaking to some of my closest friends, I found myself suddenly wanting to build a career within Web Development. After being in management and captaincy roles, I knew I loved and thrived being in a working but competitive enviroment.

I have previously played for Brentford, Aldershot and Woking FC. I am currently a Level 2 ECB cricket coach, coaching juniors and teenagers find their love for cricket!

I am familiar with HTML, HTML5 and CSS. I would love for you check out some of my projects!
